Tribute To Vikram Sarabhai

Father of ISRO

Vikram Sarabhai

Here are some facts about Vikram Sarabhai

  • Born on 12 August 1919 in Ahmedabad, Gujarat
  • Studied at the University of Cambridge
  • Worked as a professor at the Indian Institute of Science
  • Founded the Physical Research Laboratory in Ahmedabad
  • Founded the Indian Space Research Organisation
  • Received the Padma Bhushan in 1963
  • Died on 30 December 1971 in Ahmedabad, Gujarat
  • His ashes were scattered in space by the Indian Space Research Organisation
  • His birthday is celebrated as National Science Day in India
